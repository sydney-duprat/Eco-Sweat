Our commitment at EcoSweat

Authenticity

At Ecosweat, authenticity is our first value. It has its place firstly in our team, where transparency and communication are essential, and secondly in our marketing strategy, based on your impressions and our commitments. The composition and origin of our products are clearly displayed and explained with the purchase of each product. The most important thing for us is your satisfaction.

The Entrepreneurship Spirit

Ecosweat was born 5 years ago from the association of 4 friends from business schools. As novices in this field, we wanted to create a project that resembled us and above all, we were driven by a real mission: to change the way we dress. The entrepreneurial spirit for us means: to have autonomy in our work by personally leading all our projects, and to take initiatives with even more ambition for Ecosweat.

Solidarity

In developing Ecosweat, we have built relationships of trust and respect with our suppliers and designers. Our wish is to protect these small craftsmen and their work by providing them with fair remuneration and by promoting them. Fair trade is still too little known, we would like to bring it to the forefront.
