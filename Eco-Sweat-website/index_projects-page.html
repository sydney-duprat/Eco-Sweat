What's next for EcoSweat?

Creation of our own line of unisex sustainable shoes

Made in France, these new unisex sneakers are made from natural rubber, cotton scraps and recycled polyester from plastic bottles.

Size : From 36 to 48

Launching date : January 2021

Partnership with the eco-responsible fashion label "SloWeAre"

The SloWeAre label is an eco-responsible label that allows the consumer to easily identify fashion brands committed to an eco-friendly approach.

For several months now, EcoSweat has been involved with the platform and will soon be taking part in its first EcoFashionTour: a shopping session aimed at all those who wish to renew their wardrobe at their own pace by favouring eco-responsible brands and fashion designers.

Next SloWeAre "EcoFashionTour" : 03 February 2021
